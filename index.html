Our Food

Please find below certain ideas of food and cuisines our chefs have made

Shawarma

Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern street food made of thinly sliced marinated meat (often chicken or beef) cooked on a vertical rotisserie. It's typically served in a pita bread wrap with vegetables, pickles, and tahini sauce.

Indian Curry

A rich and flavorful curry featuring aromatic spices, creamy coconut milk, and your choice of tender chicken, lamb, or vegetables.

Italian Pasta

Freshly made pasta with your choice of classic sauces, from creamy pesto to hearty Bolognese, all crafted with authentic Italian ingredients.

General Tao

General Tao chicken is a popular Chinese-American dish consisting of crispy fried chicken pieces coated in a sweet and slightly spicy sauce, often made with soy sauce, ginger, garlic, and chili peppers.

Burger

A burger is a sandwich made of a cooked patty of ground meat (usually beef) placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll.

Tacos

Tacos are a popular Mexican dish consisting of a tortilla (corn or flour) filled with various ingredients, such as meat, beans, cheese, vegetables, and salsa.
